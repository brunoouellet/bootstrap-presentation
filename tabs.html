About Pivot88

Founded in 2009, Pivot88 has come a long way. We originated from the classic innovation piece of our founder not being able to find what he was looking for. Having previously launched a sourcing office in China for one of the Toyota Group companies, Stephane Boivin faced challenges of transparency and manufacturing quality and sourcing in remote locations, specifically in Asia. When he discovered that there was nothing on the market to fulfill his needs, he created Pivot88. Initially our 360° supply chain visibility and sustainability tool was wrapped up in other consulting services and delivered to environments with remote quality management issues. However, in 2012, Pivot88 was separated out as unique solution, and has developed into a full suite of trusted cloud-based supply chain quality management and sustainability tools.

Our mission and vision

We exist to help brands, retailers, factories and agents to reduce costs and standardize and improve quality. Pivot 88’s cloud-based business intelligence and analytics provide its clients with full visibility and integration of the entire process and data, allowing them to audit their supply chain in real time. We stand behind our software and processes completely, so ask us about our guarantee.
